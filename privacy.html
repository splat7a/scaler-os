Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 27, 2024

1. About Scaler-OS

Scaler-OS is operated by a direct advertiser that uses Amazon's advertising and selling partner APIs to manage our own e-commerce business operations. We do not provide services to third parties or collect data from external users.

2. Our Use of Amazon APIs

We use the following Amazon APIs exclusively for our own business operations:

  • Amazon Advertising API: To automate, scale, and optimize our advertising campaigns and reporting
  • Amazon Selling Partner API (SP-API): To manage our product listings, inventory, and seller account operations

All API usage is in accordance with Amazon's Terms of Service and API usage policies.

3. Data We Access

Through Amazon's APIs, we access data related to our own business accounts, including:

  • Our advertising campaign performance and metrics
  • Our product catalog and inventory information
  • Our sales data and order information
  • Our business analytics and reports

We do not access, collect, or process data from other sellers, advertisers, or end consumers.

4. How We Use Data

Data accessed through Amazon APIs is used solely for:

  • Managing and optimizing our own advertising campaigns
  • Analyzing our business performance
  • Managing our inventory and product listings
  • Making strategic business decisions
  • Generating reports for internal use

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the data we access through Amazon APIs. All data is stored securely and used in compliance with Amazon's data protection requirements.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (such as cloud hosting providers) to support our operations. Any such services are carefully selected to ensure they meet appropriate security and privacy standards.

7. No Consumer Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process personally identifiable information from end consumers. Our operations are limited to managing our own business accounts and data through Amazon's platforms.

8. Cookies and Tracking

This website uses minimal tracking. We do not use advertising cookies or share data with advertising networks.

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@scaler-os.com

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when this policy was last revised.
